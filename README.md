# $KHETI-SATHI$- Real-field Image Plant Disease Dataset

**Contributors:** Dr. Neeta Nain, Sagar Mal Mitharwal and Dr.  \
**Institute:** Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur \

Developed the \textbf{largest} and \textbf{first real-field (India)} plant disease dataset comprising $3,500$ images of \textbf{Citrus:} (Sweet Orange, Thai Lemon, Kagzi Lemon), covering \textbf{diseases} such as greening, anthracnose, black spot, kenkar, \textbf{novel annotation:} nutrient deficiency, and mold.
	\item Expert \textbf{ground-truth} annotations, including soil health, humidity, nutrient deficiency, and pathological reports \textbf{validated} by the Rajasthan Agriculture Research Institute \textbf{(RARI)}, Durgapura, Jaipur.
